01 About

I am a New Zealand-registered Early Childhood Education teacher and a PhD candidate in the Faculty of Education at the University of Canterbury. My doctoral research examines bilingual literacy practices in Chinese–New Zealand family homes, focusing on parent–child interaction and digital/AI-mediated learning. Alongside my research, I bring hands-on ECE teaching experience and a strong interest in translating research into practical, family- and classroom-facing tools.
