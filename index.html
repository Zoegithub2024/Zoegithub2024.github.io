About / Research

I am a PhD candidate in the Faculty of Education at the University of Canterbury, New Zealand. My research explores bilingual literacy practices within Chinese–New Zealand families, focusing on how parents and children engage in reading and language activities at home, and how digital tools mediate bilingual (Chinese–English) literacy development. My work combines qualitative case-study methods with a strong interest in family language policy and digital/AI-mediated learning.
