01 About关于我

Registered early childhood teacher and tertiary Initial Teacher Education academic with over twenty years of teaching experience across New Zealand, China, and the United Kingdom. I hold a Master of Teaching Early Childhood Education with Distinction and a current Full Practising Certificate with the Teaching Council of Aotearoa New Zealand (Registration No. 412284).

My teaching has spanned tertiary Initial Teacher Education, ECE centre-based practice, and secondary-level English and literacy instruction. Alongside this, I am a part-time PhD candidate in Education at the University of Canterbury, researching digitally mediated bilingual literacy practices in Chinese–New Zealand family homes — work that informs how I support multilingual and culturally diverse learners in tertiary and centre-based settings.

Based in Auckland; open to long-term education roles, including in Christchurch / Canterbury.

注册幼儿教师，高等院校教师教育学者，拥有二十余年跨新西兰、中国与英国的教学经验。持有优异等级的 幼儿教育硕士学位（Master of Teaching Early Childhood Education, Distinction），并持有新西兰教师 注册委员会（Teaching Council of Aotearoa New Zealand）颁发的现行执业证书（注册号 412284）。

我的教学经历涵盖高等院校教师教育、幼儿园一线教学，以及中学英语与读写教学。与此同时，我是坎特伯雷 大学教育学院的兼读制博士生，研究方向为中新家庭中数字媒介支持下的双语读写实践——这项研究也持续 影响着我在高等院校与幼儿园场景中支持多语言、多元文化学习者的方式。

现居奥克兰；愿意考虑包括基督城/坎特伯雷地区在内的长期教育类职位。

02 Teaching Philosophy教学理念

Every learner belongs.
Every voice matters.
Every step counts. 每一个学习者都有归属。
每一种声音都被听见。
每一步成长都算数。

🌱 Belonging🌱 归属感

Belonging归属感

Every learner brings a place, a language and a story. I build inclusive, Te Whāriki- and Te Tiriti-informed environments where children, families and adult learners feel they belong before they're asked to achieve.

每一位学习者都带着自己的成长背景、语言与故事而来。我致力于建立包容、体现 Te Whāriki 与 Te Tiriti 精神的学习环境，让儿童、家庭与成人学习者首先感受到归属，然后才被要求"达成"。
🗣️ Voice🗣️ 声音

Voice声音

Twenty years across New Zealand, China and the United Kingdom — and research into bilingual family literacy — have shown me that multilingual learners teach us as much as we teach them. I make space for every language and every voice.

二十年辗转新西兰、中国与英国的经历，以及对双语家庭读写的研究，让我深刻体会到：多语言学习者 教会我们的，并不比我们教给他们的少。我努力为每一种语言、每一种声音留出空间。
📈 Growth📈 成长

Growth成长

Feedback should move learners forward, not just judge them. I give clear, criteria-based, constructive feedback — and hold my own practice to the same standard: reflective, evidence-informed, always improving.

反馈应该推动学习者向前，而不只是评判他们。我提供清晰、基于评分标准、具建设性的反馈——也用 同样的标准要求自己：保持反思、以证据为依据、持续精进。

Research Project研究项目

Case studies of Chinese migrant families in New Zealand using AI tools to support their children's bilingual reading and language learning at home — documenting methods, challenges and observations from real parent–child interactions, as a reference for other overseas bilingual families.

记录新西兰华人移民家庭如何运用 AI 工具，支持孩子在家进行中英双语阅读与语言学习的真实案例—— 梳理其中的方法、挑战与观察心得，为海外双语家庭提供参考。

Thesis毕业论文

Understanding the Educational Aspirations of Two Preschool Chinese Migrant Parents in One ECE Centre关于两位学前华人移民家长教育期望的研究：以一所幼儿教育中心为例

Master of Teaching Early Childhood Education, Distinction — Toi Ohomai Institute of Technology, 2021幼儿教育硕士（优异）— Toi Ohomai 理工学院，2021年

A qualitative case study exploring how two Chinese migrant parents in Tauranga understood their preschoolers' learning, and the language, cultural and power-imbalance barriers shaping their engagement with an ECE centre — with implications for culturally responsive home–centre partnership.

一项质性案例研究，探讨陶朗加两位华人移民家长如何理解学龄前子女的学习，以及语言、文化与权力 不对等因素如何影响他们与幼儿园的互动——为建立具文化回应性的家园合作关系提供启示。
